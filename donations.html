Why Donate With Us?

Make a difference in your community and the environment by donating items you no longer need.

  • Convenient home pickup service

  • Support for local charities and families

  • Reduce landfill waste and promote reuse

What items can I donate?

We accept gently used furniture, clothing, appliances, electronics, books, toys, and household goods. Please ensure items are clean and in working condition.

How do I schedule a donation pickup?

You can schedule a pickup online or by calling our office. Choose a convenient date and time, and our team will handle the rest.

Are there items you do not accept?

We cannot accept hazardous materials, broken items, or mattresses. If you have questions about specific items, please contact us.

Who receives my donated items?

Your donations are distributed to local charities and organizations that support families and individuals in need.
